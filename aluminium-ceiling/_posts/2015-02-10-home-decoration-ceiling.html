TOBOND Aluminium Ceiling

TOBOND Aluminium Ceiling is made of high quality aluminium sheet with excellent surface coating. It is widely used for indoor ceiling designs and public places ceiling. TOBOND Aluminum Ceiling is with prime performance of economic efficiency, environment friendly and flexible installation.

The series include plates of various sizes, all of which can be combined alternatively to make abundant effects.


ADVANTAGES OF TOBOND Aluminum Ceiling

1.Surface smoothness and easy cleaning

2.Flexible suspension system makes each ceiling easily installed and disconnected

3.Easy to match lamps or other ceiling parts

4.Surface color can be stable for more than 15 years by indoor use

5.Function: Mould proof, Fireproof, Waterproof, easy to clean


TOBOND Aluminum Ceiling Application

• Pop Aluminum ceiling tiles for hall

• Aluminum ceiling designs for shops

• Different types aluminium ceiling

• Replacemant of old public places

• Balconies, Kitchen and bath room ceiling

• Industrial usage, vehicle bodies
