TOBOND FIREPROOF ALUMINIUM COMPOSITE PANEL

TOBOND FIREPROOF Aluminium Composite Panel is one new innovation and creation in the building decoration panel fields. Its excellent fireproof performance comes from the natural inorganic mineral material middle core .And TOBOND FR ACP is going to be a new trend of curtain wall cladding field, and become a new replacement of traditional stone and heavy metal wall cladding panels.

The reaction to fire classification of TOBOND brand FR Aluminium Composite Panel is :A2,S1,d0,t0.

ADVANTAGES OF TOBOND ACP

1.High strength, easy maintenance, 20 years not color fade.

2.Easy fabrication and installation.

3.Good Impact Resistance.

4.Light weight and rigid, reducing buildings load, good performance for quakeproof.

5.Good Smoothingness.

6.Fireproof grade: A1, B2

7.Various color and size for option.
